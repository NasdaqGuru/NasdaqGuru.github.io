Section I · Executive Overview

From Static Reports to Live Strategic Awareness

This report adds a separate alert layer to the foresight, portfolio, and HKS stewardship reports. It is designed to identify news or public-data changes that should trigger a project review, campaign opportunity review, donor-risk check, pledge follow-up, or proposal-timing adjustment.

24 hrs

Initial Triage

High-materiality signals are reviewed within one business day and routed to the right owner.

6

Signal Families

Policy, markets, AI regulation, reputation, research, and peer campaign activity.

4

Action Paths

Advance, pause, reframe, or steward based on impact, confidence, urgency, and owner fit.

Operating thesis

An opportunity can become campaign-ready because of an external event: a federal budget change, a liquidity window, a research breakthrough, a peer institution move, or a reputational moment. AI-assisted news tracking makes those moments visible early enough for advancement leaders to act.

Section III · Automation Workflow

How the Alert Engine Moves From News to Action

The workflow is designed for human review at decision points. AI accelerates reading, classification, scoring, and draft routing; leadership still owns judgment.

01 · Ingest

Public Signals

RSS, APIs, public filings, grant notices, market data, school news, and peer campaign releases.

02 · Classify

Topic Match

NLP tags each item by project theme, school, donor segment, region, and campaign priority.

03 · Score

Materiality

Urgency, confidence, impact, opportunity/risk direction, and recommended action.

04 · Route

Owner Queue

AVP, VP, gift officer, prospect research, school partner, or campaign strategy team.

05 · Act

Decision

Advance, pause, reframe, steward, or watch based on owner review.

ScoreMeaningExpected Action
80-100Material signalLikely to affect a major opportunity, donor strategy, proposal language, or project timing.Route within 24 hours; create briefing note; assign owner.
50-79Watch signalRelevant but not immediately decisive; useful for weekly portfolio review.Include in weekly digest; monitor for follow-up signals.
0-49Reference signalContextual background with low direct impact.Archive to searchable signal library.
