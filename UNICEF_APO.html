High
Sustained global crises (Gaza, Ukraine) risk donor fatigue, reducing emergency campaign response rates by an estimated 12–18% in FY2026 vs. FY2024 peak.
Mitigation: Scenario model with base/downside built into FY2026 budget; flexible EOY creative strategy on standby.
FundraiseUp Platform Disruption
High
Any platform outage or misconfiguration during EOY or Giving Tuesday window could cause material attribution gaps and revenue reporting errors (est. $2–5M impact window).
Mitigation: Pre-launch validation checklist + real-time monitoring protocol + Technology escalation path documented.
Power BI DAX Reconciliation Gap
High
3.8% Power BI↔BBCRM discrepancy driven by DAX measure-level filter inconsistencies. Risk of VP-level QBR presenting incorrect figures if unresolved.
Mitigation: Q1 FY2026 data model audit with Technology; interim reconciliation note added to all affected dashboards.
Direct Mail CPA Escalation
Medium
Direct mail CPA rising from $75 to $82 over 5 years with declining response rates. If trend continues, DM becomes cash-flow negative in 2–3 years without file optimization.
Mitigation: ZIP suppression model + audience tier testing proposed for Q2 FY2026 with vendor collaboration.
Lapsed Donor Email Hygiene
Medium
12% of lapsed donor records (357K file) lack valid email addresses, limiting win-back program reach and depressing reactivation rate from theoretical 12% to actual 8.1%.
Mitigation: Email append vendor evaluation (Melissa Data / Epsilon) planned Q3 FY2026; est. 8–10% match rate.
DRTV Incrementality Uncertainty
Medium
DRTV declining 5 consecutive years with 38.5-month break-even. Lack of MMM study means true brand-halo value is unmeasured, risking either over- or under-investment.
Mitigation: MMM study commission budgeted for Q1 FY2026 with Technology; hold DRTV budget flat pending results.
GL Reporting Latency
Low
Monthly GL close cycle creates 30-day reporting lag, occasionally causing dashboard figures to differ from Finance reports during close period.
Mitigation: Data latency caveat documented in all dashboards; Finance reconciliation SLA established.
Attrition Above Benchmark
Low
Annual attrition at 37.6% (vs. 35% sector benchmark) primarily driven by one-time emergency donors not converting to recurring.
Mitigation: 45-day emergency donor 2nd-gift cultivation track planned Q1 FY2026; projected to recover 8–10K donors/yr.
Attribution Model Transition
Low
Transition from last-click to data-driven attribution (65% complete) may shift reported channel credit allocation, creating confusion at channel-level QBRs.
Mitigation: Parallel reporting during transition period; stakeholder briefing scheduled for Q2 FY2026.
Bezos Match Tracking Complexity
Medium
$500M Bezos match requires separate revenue tracking line in both BBCRM and GL to avoid double-counting. Current data architecture not ready for this structure.
Mitigation: Q1 FY2026 architecture design sprint with Finance + Technology; Bezos match to be tracked as separate fund code.
