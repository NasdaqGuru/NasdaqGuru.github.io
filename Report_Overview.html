Paired Operational Reports and Progress Consoles

_Report.html files are operational reports. _Console.html files are VERITAS AI/campaign progress monitors. Each school keeps both files side by side so readers can move between operating detail and transformation progress without confusion.

Central Pair

The central pair sets the pattern for every school: VP_Report is the overall operational report; AAD_UDAS_Console is the central VERITAS progress monitor.
