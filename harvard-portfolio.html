Section V

Metrics + Resilience

This section clarifies the numbers that previously felt vague or suspicious.

What 84% Means

84% is modeled signal precision: the share of AI-recommended prospects whose readiness signal matched a verified gift-officer next step. It is not a close rate.

What $63M Means

$63M is a modeled flexible-fund shortfall after restricted payout and expected PPF coverage are applied to FY2026 operating/research-continuity needs. It is not an audited budget number.

Tier 3 / Long Horizon

Tier 3 is not immediate liquidity. It is a stewardship pool for donors with illiquid assets, estate plans, or future conversion events.

Tier 1 · Liquid

DAFs, marketable securities, treasury positions. Potential deployment within 30 days.

Tier 2 · Semi-Liquid

Lock-up expirations, business transactions, real estate sales. Structured commitment within 90-180 days.

Tier 3 · Long Horizon

Private company equity, estate intentions, art/real assets. Keep warm, steward carefully, do not count as crisis cash.
